Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2025  ·  Effective: March 2025
Home
VYAYAM is committed to protecting your personal and health data in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) of India.
1. Data Controller

VYAYAM ("we", "us", "our") operates this platform. For any data-related queries, contact us at privacy@vyayam.in.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data when you register and use VYAYAM:

  • Identity data: name, age, biological sex (optional)
  • Contact data: phone number, email address (optional)
  • Health data: height, weight, training history, injury/pain history, fitness assessments, exercise results, hormonal cycle information (optional), and nutrition data (optional)
  • Usage data: session logs, exercise performance, form scores, and progress metrics
  • Device data: camera frames processed locally on your device for real-time form analysis (frames are not transmitted to or stored on our servers)
3. Purpose and Legal Basis

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • Delivering personalised physiotherapy and strength training programmes (contractual necessity)
  • Tracking progress and adjusting prescriptions over time (legitimate interest / consent)
  • Enabling therapist review and override of your programme (consent)
  • Nutrition tracking and meal planning (consent)
  • Safety and medical screening to prevent harm (vital interest)

Health data is sensitive personal data under DPDPA. We process it only on the basis of your explicit, informed consent given at registration.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Your assigned physiotherapist / coach — to review and adjust your programme
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — for secure hosting (data remains within India where feasible)
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or court order
5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, plus 3 years thereafter for audit and safety purposes, unless you request deletion earlier. Health data is deleted within 30 days of a verified deletion request.

6. Your Rights (DPDPA 2023)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — obtain a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and data
  • Right to grievance redressal — raise a complaint through our grievance mechanism
  • Right to nominate — nominate a person to exercise rights on your behalf in case of incapacity

To exercise any right, email privacy@vyayam.in or use the Delete My Account feature in the app.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data storage, hashed passwords, HTTPS-only access, and rate-limited authentication endpoints. Camera data for pose analysis is processed entirely on your device and never leaves it.

8. Cookies

We use session cookies (essential, not tracking) to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children

VYAYAM is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has registered, contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified by in-app notice at least 15 days in advance.

11. Contact / Grievance Officer

Grievance Officer: VYAYAM Privacy Team
Email: privacy@vyayam.in
Response time: within 72 hours of receipt
