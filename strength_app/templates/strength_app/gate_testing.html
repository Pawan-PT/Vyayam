How It Works

For each movement pattern you'll start at the easiest variation. If it's too easy, you click "Too Easy → Try Next Level" and we advance you up the ladder. When you find your level, click "This is My Level" — we record it and prescribe at that exact exercise. No fixed list. No assumptions. Your prescription matches exactly where YOU are today.

Tips for Accurate Results
  • Be honest — this determines your programme
  • Don't push through real pain (soreness is fine)
  • If unsure, pick the lower level — you can always advance
  • Use the camera if available — it counts reps automatically
  • Manual entry is perfectly fine if no camera
