Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: March 2025

Important: Read this disclaimer carefully before using any exercise programme generated by VYAYAM.
Not Medical Advice

VYAYAM is an AI-assisted fitness and physiotherapy support tool. The exercise prescriptions, recommendations, and content provided by this platform are for general informational and fitness purposes only. They do not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult a qualified medical professional or registered physiotherapist before beginning any new exercise programme, especially if you have any pre-existing medical conditions, injuries, or health concerns.

Inherent Risk of Exercise

Physical exercise involves an inherent risk of injury. By using VYAYAM, you acknowledge and accept that:

  • Exercise may carry risk even when performed with correct form.
  • Programmes generated by AI may not be suitable for every individual in every circumstance.
  • You are solely responsible for exercising within your own limits and capabilities.
Stop Immediately If You Experience
  • Chest pain, tightness, or pressure
  • Severe shortness of breath
  • Dizziness, fainting, or light-headedness
  • Sharp or severe joint / muscle pain
  • Numbness or tingling in limbs
  • Any symptom that feels unusual or alarming

Call emergency services (112 in India) if symptoms are severe.

AI Limitations

The AI algorithms powering VYAYAM are trained on population-level data and may not account for individual anatomical variations, rare medical conditions, or unusual circumstances. The real-time form analysis uses computer vision and may not detect all form errors. Always prioritise how your body feels over what the system reports.

No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Use of VYAYAM does not create a doctor-patient or physiotherapist-patient relationship. If you have been linked to a physiotherapist through the platform, that therapist acts in a coaching / advisory capacity within the app; this does not constitute formal clinical care.

Pregnancy and Post-Natal

If you are pregnant or post-natal, do not use exercise programmes generated by VYAYAM without explicit clearance from your obstetrician or midwife.

Limitation of Liability

VYAYAM, its founders, employees, and partner therapists shall not be liable for any injury, health complication, or adverse outcome arising from the use of content or programmes on this platform.
